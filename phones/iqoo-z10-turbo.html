iQOO Z10 Turbo — ৳34,000 (Unofficial)
Released: April 28, 2025  ·  China ROM only
⚠ Critical Warning — China ROM Only
This phone has no global version. What you get in BD is the Chinese ROM running OriginOS 5 — not a global Android build.
No Google Play preinstalled — you must sideload it manually. Works but some Google apps behave oddly on China ROM.
5G bands may not work in BD — China ROM 5G bands (n41/n78/n79) may not match BD towers. 4G works fine. Verify with your seller before buying.
No official after-sales support in BD — iQOO has no authorized service center here. If hardware breaks, you are on your own.
Bootloader: Not officially unlockable — OriginOS bootloader lock has no public unlock method available outside China.
⚠ Known Issues
Persistent Overheating under extended gaming + charging
Back panel gets hot after 1hr+ gaming especially while charging. Reported by multiple users. 7K vapor chamber helps but Dimensity 8400 generates real heat under sustained load.
Persistent Battery drain faster than expected under heavy use
Despite 7620mAh, heavy gaming drains faster than the battery size suggests. Dimensity 8400 is power hungry at peak load. SOT under heavy use reported at 5–6hrs not the advertised figure.
Software Device freeze / stuck on boot screen
Some units freeze on wake or get stuck on boot. Requires force restart. Reported across OriginOS 5 builds. Usually resolves with restart but recurs.
Software Google apps behave oddly on China ROM
Google Play sideloaded works but some Play Store apps trigger verification errors. YouTube, Gmail work fine. Banking apps may not pass SafetyNet/Play Integrity checks.
Software OriginOS update pushes cause instability
OTA updates from China servers can cause instability if you are outside China. Recommended to block OTA updates after setting up the phone to avoid random regressions.
Hardware No dead pixel reports — display quality consistent
No widespread dead pixel or display defect reports found. Display quality appears reliable across user reports.

⚠ Known Issues & Persistent Bugs
Persistent Multitouch failure under gaming load
Touch response throttles after extended gaming — affects edge inputs badly. Reported widely on GSMArena. POCO pushed a keyboard layout hack instead of a real fix. Still present as of early 2026.
Persistent Battery drain + overheating after HyperOS 3 update
Multiple users report standby drain of 10–15% overnight after updating. Overheating during camera use and benchmarks even outside gaming. Animation fix was pushed but battery drain persists.
Persistent Lock screen freezing on wake
Phone freezes on lock screen in the morning requiring restart. Reported in long-term reviews and forums. No confirmed fix yet.
Software Network signal drops — airplane mode workaround needed
Weak/slow network requires toggling airplane mode to restore signal. Reported on HyperOS 3. Affects both 4G and 5G.
Software Speaker silence after HyperOS 3 update
Main speaker goes completely silent after HyperOS 3 update for some units. Reported multiple times on GSMArena. Xiaomi has not officially acknowledged.
Software Aggressive RAM management despite 12GB
HyperOS kills background apps even with 12GB RAM. Long-term review confirms system acts too aggressively. Partially manageable via developer options.
Hardware Plastic frame transfers heat unpleasantly
Under gaming or heavy use, plastic frame conducts heat to hands directly. Not a safety issue but uncomfortable for long sessions. No fix possible — hardware design.
Hardware Front camera quality regression vs X6 Pro
Multiple X6 Pro owners report selfie quality downgrade on X7 Pro. Blurry, noisy low-light selfies and video. No software fix has resolved this.
